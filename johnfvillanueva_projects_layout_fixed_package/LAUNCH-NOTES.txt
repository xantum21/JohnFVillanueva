JOHN VILLANUEVA WEBSITE - LAUNCH NOTES

WHAT WAS ADDED
- Canonical URLs, Open Graph metadata, Twitter card metadata, Person/WebSite JSON-LD
- Social sharing image, favicon, Apple touch icon, and web manifest
- sitemap.xml, robots.txt, and 404.html
- Privacy-conscious public resume PDF on the Work page
- Externalized embedded images for browser caching and smaller HTML files
- Image dimensions, decoding hints, and lazy loading below the fold
- Optional security headers file for Netlify / Cloudflare Pages

PRIVACY DECISIONS
- No phone number, home address, date of birth, CNA license number, or exact current workplace is published.
- The public resume identifies the current role only as an East Bay skilled nursing facility.
- The contact email is visually masked and assembled by JavaScript to reduce basic scraping.
- Social and gaming profiles remain linked because they were intentionally supplied for public use.
- No analytics, cookies, ad pixels, or visitor tracking were added.

DEPLOYMENT
1. Upload every file and the assets folder without changing the relative structure.
2. Make index.html the homepage.
3. Configure 404.html as the custom 404 page if the host supports it.
4. Confirm https://johnfvillanueva.com/sitemap.xml and /robots.txt are reachable.
5. Test the social card with LinkedIn Post Inspector and another messaging platform after deployment.
6. If your host does not support the _headers file, copy the listed headers into its security/header settings.

EXTERNAL MINI-PROJECTS
The live quiz and game pages at /dino-racers, /pharmacology, /math-game, /spanish, and /filipino are not part of this ZIP. Their source pages must be updated separately if you want the portfolio header/footer added to them.
