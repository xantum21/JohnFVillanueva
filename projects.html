Dino Kart Racer

A prehistoric combat racer built for the browser, featuring rival AI, power-ups, collisions, audio feedback, and a trilobite projectile that behaves with far more determination than its fossil record would suggest.

  • Combines game-state logic, interface design, AI behavior, collision systems, and iterative balancing
  • Evolved through repeated playtesting rather than a single static generation
  • Represents the fun side of the same build–test–revise loop used in more practical tools
