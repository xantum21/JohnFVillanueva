About John · California rooted, globally curious

I’ve never been very good at being only one thing.

I’m John Villanueva—a BSN candidate, hands-on caregiver, business graduate, builder, gamer, traveler, and lifelong learner. My path has crossed marketing, business banking, entrepreneurship, technology, and healthcare. The common thread is simple: I care about people, I like understanding how things work, and I’m happiest when I’m building toward something meaningful.

The short story

The path looks nonlinear. The values are not.

I did not follow a perfectly straight line from one title to the next—and I am glad I didn’t. Each chapter gave me a different way to understand people, systems, and what dependable work actually looks like.

I started as the kind of student who joined organizations, hosted events, competed, learned languages, and chased whatever made the world feel larger. I later studied marketing and Japanese language and culture, worked in business banking, explored entrepreneurship, and kept building projects on the side.

Healthcare raised the stakes. In direct patient care, empathy has to become action: show up, pay attention, communicate clearly, protect dignity, and be dependable when someone needs you. After more than 1,000 hours of hands-on care, I’m finishing my BSN and preparing for the next chapter as a nurse.

Business taught me to think in systems. Healthcare taught me that systems only matter when they serve people. Building things lets me connect the two.

Best at Being Himself

I’m serious about building a future. I’m equally serious about remaining a person.
